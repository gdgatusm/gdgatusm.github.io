About The Tournament

This isn't just a game; it's a fusion of learning, entertainment, and cutting-edge tech. A joint effort by the Google Developer Groups (GDG), the Cyber Innovation Lab, and the School of Computing to build a vibrant community and showcase incredible talent.

We're even broadcasting matches live into real-time Gaussian Splat viewers, so you can watch in VR! How cool is that?
